Eleven
A young girl with psychokinetic abilities who escaped from Hawkins National Laboratory. She becomes close friends with Mike and the other boys.
Mike Wheeler
The leader of the group of friends who first discovers Eleven. He is loyal, brave, and deeply cares for his friends.
Will Byers
Mike's friend who mysteriously disappears in the first season, taken to the Upside Down. He is sensitive and artistic.
Dustin Henderson
A member of the friend group known for his intelligence and cleidocranial dysplasia, which affects his teeth. He provides comic relief.
Lucas Sinclair
The pragmatic and skeptical member of the group, initially wary of Eleven but eventually becomes one of her strongest allies.
Jonathan Byers
Will's older brother, a quiet and introspective young man who is passionate about photography and cares deeply for his family.
Jim Hopper
The chief of police in Hawkins who initially investigates Will's disappearance and becomes a father figure to Eleven.
Steve Harrington
Initially a popular high school student, he evolves into a caring and protective figure for the younger kids.
Nancy Wheeler
Mike's older sister who becomes involved in the supernatural events in Hawkins and develops into a strong, independent young woman.
Joyce Byers
Will's determined mother who stops at nothing to find her son when he goes missing, displaying incredible resilience.

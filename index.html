PLOT

Learn More

    In the seemingly tranquil town of Hawkins, Indiana, a cascade of inexplicable supernatural phenomena begins to unravel, gradually exposing a clandestine gateway that bridges our familiar reality with the terrifying parallel dimension known as the Upside Down.
    This mysterious portal, initially concealed beneath the guise of ordinary small-town life, becomes the epicenter of increasingly disturbing events that challenge the very fabric of space and time.

The Disappearance Case

Will Byers' vanishing sparks Hawkins' largest search. His abandoned bicycle and strange clues suggest supernatural forces.

Parallel Dimensions

Hawkins Lab experiments tear open a gateway to the Upside Down—a dark mirror dimension.

Government Conspiracy

Authorities conceal the lab's dangerous experiments, manipulating evidence and silencing witnesses.

    A courageous band of young adolescents, bound by unbreakable friendship and unwavering determination, joins forces with a mysterious girl possessing extraordinary psychic abilities to confront the nightmarish entities emerging from an alien dimension.

Eleven

Escaped test subject with powerful telekinetic abilities. Codenamed 011, she evolves from laboratory experiment to heroic protector.

Mike Wheeler

The group's loyal leader whose unwavering friendship provides emotional anchor. His compassion and determination unite the team.

Max Mayfield

The resilient newcomer with a tough exterior masking deep vulnerability. Her skateboarding skills and sharp wit hide traumatic family struggles.

Dustin Henderson

The scientific genius with sharp wit and technical expertise. His intelligence and resourcefulness often provide crucial solutions.
